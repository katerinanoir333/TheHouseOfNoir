Privacy notice

Last updated: July 18, 2026

What is collected

When you present yourself, we collect your name, email address, your introduction and any optional Instagram username or discovery information you provide. The entry gate stores your language preference on your device and remembers age confirmation for the current browser session.

How it is used

This information is used only to assess your request, verify reasonable authenticity, protect The House and reply if there is reason to continue. It is not sold or published.

Service provider

The request form is processed by Formspree. Its handling of submitted data is also governed by its own privacy terms. The operators of The House receive the submission through that service.

Retention and your choices

Submissions should be kept only for as long as reasonably needed to evaluate or manage the conversation. You may ask for access, correction or deletion by replying to an official message you receive from The House. Avoid submitting information you do not want processed.

Security and adults only

No internet transmission is risk-free. Never send passwords, recovery phrases, financial account details, identity documents or account credentials. This site is intended only for adults aged 18 or older; the age gate is a self-declaration and does not independently verify identity.

Important limitation

This notice is a plain-language operational statement, not jurisdiction-specific legal advice. The site owner should have it reviewed by qualified counsel if local law, business practices or data handling changes.
