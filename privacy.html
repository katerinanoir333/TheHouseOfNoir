Privacy notice

Last updated: July 2026

What we collect

When you request consideration, we collect your real name, Instagram username, email address and, if you choose to provide it, your WhatsApp number.

Why we collect it

We use this information only to assess your request, verify that you are a real person, contact you about the process and protect the integrity of The House.

Who receives it

Your submission is delivered privately to the authorized operators of The House of Noir through our form-processing provider. We do not sell your information or publish it.

Retention and requests

Information is retained only while it is reasonably needed to evaluate or manage the relationship. You may ask for access, correction or deletion by replying to an official message you receive from The House.

Security

No internet transmission is completely risk-free. Never send passwords, recovery phrases, payment-card details or account credentials through the request form or by direct message.
