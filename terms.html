Terms of passage

Last updated: July 2026

A request is not an acceptance

Submitting your information begins a private consideration process. It does not create an obligation, reservation, contract or guarantee of contact or an encounter.

Offerings

If The House chooses to continue, you may receive three offering options privately. The applicable value, method, purpose and conditions must be disclosed in that message before you decide. Do not present an offering based on instructions from an unofficial account.

Safety

The House will never ask for a password, cryptocurrency recovery phrase, login credential or unrestricted access to an account. Gift-card details should be shared only through the specifically confirmed channel and only after independently verifying the sender.

Encounters

Any future experience is bespoke and subject to separate discussion, mutual agreement, boundaries, availability and applicable law. No intimate or unlawful service is advertised or promised by this website.

Right to decline

Either party may end the process before a separately agreed experience. Any refund or credit terms associated with an offering must be stated before that offering is made.
