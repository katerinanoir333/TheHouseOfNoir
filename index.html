01 / THE HOUSE

Not a catalogue.
A point of view.

The House exists for encounters that cannot be reduced to a menu. Each begins with context, conversation and mutual trust.

Discretion is expected. Boundaries are respected. Details are shared only when there is reason to continue.

don’t presume.
